# **App Name**: Nairobi Dental

## Core Features:

- Interactive Homepage: Showcases key services, team highlights, and calls to action with an elegant, responsive layout.
- Detailed Service Pages: Provides comprehensive information about various dental treatments and procedures offered.
- Dentist & Team Profiles: Showcases the qualified dental professionals, building trust and highlighting expertise.
- Patient Testimonials Display: Features authentic patient reviews and ratings to highlight service quality.
- Online Appointment Booking: A user-friendly form for patients to schedule consultations and appointments efficiently.
- Virtual Clinic Tour: A visually appealing gallery or video showcase of the clinic's facilities and state-of-the-art equipment.
- AI Dental Care Advisor: An AI-powered tool that provides general dental health advice and answers common questions based on user queries, guiding them towards professional consultation when necessary.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, sophisticated green (#425A31) to convey health, nature, and premium quality.
- Background color: A subtle, near-white with a hint of green (#F8F9F7) to maintain cleanliness and spaciousness.
- Accent color: Warm, inviting gold (#D9B34D) to add a touch of elegance and optimism, emphasizing smiles and welcoming warmth.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for a modern, high-end, and elegant feel. Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for legibility, warmth, and contemporary readability.
- Use sleek, minimalist line icons that align with a modern and premium aesthetic. Incorporate green and gold hues for visual consistency.
- Prioritize a clean, open layout with ample whitespace to enhance the 'sleek and elegant' feel. Employ a modular grid system for a structured and harmonious visual hierarchy across all sections.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions for navigation elements, card hovers, and on-scroll content reveals to create a premium, interactive, and polished user experience.
